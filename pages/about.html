My Story

Hey there! I'm Laksh Mittal — passionate about tech, biology, and staying fit. I code during the day, hit the gym in the evening, and unwind by playing guitar or shooting creative videos.

My journey in technology began when I was young, fascinated by how computers could solve complex problems. This curiosity led me to pursue computer science and develop a deep understanding of programming languages and algorithmic thinking.

Alongside my technical interests, I maintain a strong commitment to physical fitness. The discipline required in the gym translates well to coding — both require patience, consistency, and continuous improvement.

Biology has always been another passion of mine. Understanding how living organisms function at a cellular level provides fascinating insights that sometimes inspire my technical work as well.

My Philosophy

I believe in the power of balance — between technical skill and creative expression, between physical health and mental acuity. This holistic approach guides how I work and live.

I'm constantly learning and exploring new technologies, while making sure to maintain a healthy lifestyle that fuels my creativity and productivity.
